# FAQ

## Is EVM a psychological model?
No. EVM vectors exist strictly in EVM-space and are not claims about internal states.

## Does EVM require model fine-tuning?
No. EVM is model-agnostic and does not require weight modification.

## Can I use EVM commercially?
Not without a written commercial license agreement. See COMMERCIAL_LICENSE_REQUIRED.md.
